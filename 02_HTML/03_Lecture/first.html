Yogi Adityanath (born Ajay Mohan Singh Bisht; 5 June 1972)[a] is an Indian Hindu monk and politician, belonging to the Bharatiya Janata Party who has been serving as the Chief Minister of Uttar Pradesh since 19 March 2017. He is the longest serving chief minister of Uttar Pradesh, being in office for 7 years,[6] and the only UP chief minister to have two consecutive term
